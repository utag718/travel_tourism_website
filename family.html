FAMILY TOURS
Find the right choice for your family

KERALA


Kerala, often referred to as "God's Own Country," is a southwestern state of India renowned for its enchanting natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and vibrant traditions. With its lush green landscapes, serene backwaters, and picturesque hill stations, Kerala captivates visitors with its unmatched charm.

DARJEELING


Nestled in the Himalayan foothills of West Bengal, Darjeeling is a picturesque hill station renowned for its breathtaking vistas, tea gardens, and rich cultural heritage. Perched at an elevation of about 2,000 meters, it offers a refreshing escape from the bustling city life, with its cool climate and lush greenery. Darjeeling is synonymous with its world-famous tea, cultivated in the sprawling terraced gardens that adorn its slopes.

ANDAMAN & NICOBAR ISLANDS


The Andaman and Nicobar Islands, a remote archipelago nestled in the Bay of Bengal, is a tropical paradise renowned for its pristine beaches, azure waters, and lush green forests. With its secluded coves, coral reefs, and diverse marine life, it's a haven for snorkelers and scuba divers. Home to indigenous tribes like the Sentinelese and Great Andamanese, the islands boast a rich cultural heritage.
